About Us

The project wiJa is to improve learning skills of kids while they are having fun. We are regular parents and we design our Android App with perfect content for children.

- ads free -

First of all, our app is free from advertisements and disturbing information for young kids. We are appalled by wild ads poping out in regular Android apps for kids. The content of these ads cannot be supervised by the parents. And kids are at risk and not ready for that. We stepped up against that with this wiJa's project.

learn more >>
- no jailing animations -

Secondly, a sober design was prefered to help kids to navigate easily through the whole app. WiJa's apps are not saturated with attention drawing animations. Kids are free to get in and get out of wiJa thanks to the pure design. We don't want your kids to get trapped by wild animations running around. We keep your kid focus on scheduled task. When he has finished, he may leave anytime.

- supervised content -

Lastly, we are responsible for the content of the app and the educational material within. We will like your return to improve it.
